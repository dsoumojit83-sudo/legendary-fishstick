Policies & Guidelines

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when utilizing our contact forms or communicating via email or chat. This includes your name, email address, project briefs, and custom messages. We also collect raw footage, brand assets, and content materials required to execute your project.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to provide, maintain, and deliver our video editing and motion graphics services. We also use it to communicate with you regarding project timelines, pricing options, next steps, and to process payments securely.

3. Payments & Cashback

Payment Terms: We require full payment upfront before commencing work on any project. This secures your spot in our workflow and allows us to dedicate our full resources to your edit immediately.

Cashback Policy: A valid coupon code applies up to 10% cashback. All rewards and coupon cashbacks will be sent back to the original payment source within the next 2 business days after verification.

4. Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you do not like the final delivered project and we are unable to resolve the issues through our standard revision process, we will issue a full refund. Refunds are processed back to the original payment method.

5. Data Protection and Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information or raw footage to third parties. Your project files and personal data are kept strictly confidential. Completed projects may be featured in our portfolio (such as our Featured Edits on YouTube or Instagram) only with your prior consent or once the content has been publicly published by you.
